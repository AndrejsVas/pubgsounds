Aim and how to use

The aim of this project is to help players learn and recognize the different weapon sounds present in the game. Correctly identifying the weapons shot and its range is crucial for tracking enemy combatants as well as understanding positioning and is a vital part of tactical planing.

The range slider allows to hear the weapon at different ranges and radio buttons are available to switch between firing modes and silenced mode.

Methodology

Each weapon required recording replays of the various shooting modes and silencers ingame and then recording the sounds of the replays , the crate weapons are currently missing because of the hardships of recording them , however they might appear in the future.

All audio recordings have been made ingame and some of the maximum audible ranges could be barely audible and may require adjusting your volume. The background noise present in the sound recordings is part of the ingame audio background and cannot be removed without affecting the weapon sounds.

Sound was recorded with the HRTF function on , if you play with that function off then some of the distances will sound a bit different , particularly the echoes.
